My Projects



Automatic Door Opening:

The automatic door opening systems are used in commercial buildings, shopping malls, theatres, etc. These systems are used to open the door when a person comes near to the entrance of the door and closes it after he moves away from the door or after entering into the door.







Smart Dustbin:

Smart bins are an intelligent waste management system, which operates smartly to minimize human effort and detect humans. In the era of COVID-19, people are trying to innovate everyday life things and make things as contactless as possible. Smart dustbin is one of those innovative ideas.







Student Grading System:

A grading system is a method of evaluating a child's educational performance solely on the basis of points. It will reduce the number of students who are misclassified based on their grades. A Grading System allows users to enter student information and grades at the same time, and the system will calculate the total average of the student's grades.
